Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

A classic Italian pizza with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves. Simple, delicious, and perfect for beginners to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, knead into a dough.
  3. Let the dough rest for 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  9. Top with fresh basil leaves and serve.
