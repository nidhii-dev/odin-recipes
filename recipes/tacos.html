Paneer Tacos

Description

Flavourful paneer tacos with a spiced filling, fresh toppings and a squeeze of lime. A great vegetarian twist on a classic favourite.

Ingredients

  • 6 small taco shells or tortillas
  • 300g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for topping
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add paneer cubes and cook until golden on all sides.
  3. Add cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  4. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Warm taco shells in the oven for 3-4 minutes at 180°C.
  6. Fill each shell with paneer, lettuce, tomatoes and cheese.
  7. Top with sour cream and salsa.
  8. Squeeze lime juice over the top and serve.
