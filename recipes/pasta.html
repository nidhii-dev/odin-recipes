Spaghetti Pasta

Description

A simple and delicious spaghetti with a rich tomato and garlic sauce, topped with parmesan cheese. Light, flavourful and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for topping
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce, add Italian seasoning and chili flakes.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 10 minutes on low heat.
  6. Toss spaghetti with the sauce until well coated.
  7. Serve topped with parmesan cheese.
